Magic

Sorceries in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered are a type of Magic and can be cast with a Catalyst. All Sorceries have a required amount of Intelligence that must be met in order to use, with the highest being 50 and the lowest being 10, and a specific number of Attunement slots in order to be equipped. This forces players to make tough choices with their Stats, as players opting to use high level Sorceries will have less or no points to spend on Vitality and Endurance, limiting their survivability. Weapons that scale with Intelligence (e.g. Moonlight Greatsword, or Upgraded through certain paths) will prove to be a great help when casting is not an option, or, when saving casts.
Catalysts effect the damage of Sorceries depending on the amount of Intelligence you have.

There are 4 pieces of equipment that are known to increase spell damage. (patch 1.05)
  • Head: Crown of Dusk+20% (Both)
  • Head: Crown of the Dark Sun+10% (Both)
  • Ring: Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring+ 20% (For Sorcery and Pyromancy)
  • Ring of the Sun's Firstborn +20% (For Miracles)
(combined bonus of rings with: Crown of Dusk = +40%, Crown of the Dark Sun = 30% for sorcery)
